OUR TEAM

TommComm is led by talented students who will be the future of the communications field. Students are held to the highest standards and understand the real impact their work has. Our clients deserve the very best, and we hire only innovative, strategic, principled and dependable students.

Director

Claire Rossez

Claire is a St. Thomas senior double majoring in Communication and Journalism (COJO) and English, and minoring in French. She would love to have a career that combines her passion for writing with her love of working in teams. Although she wants to get some work experience first, Claire hopes to go to grad school someday to get a Masters in either communication or business.

Being a full-time student with multiple on-campus jobs keeps Claire busy, but she enjoys spending time with friends and exploring the Twin Cities in her time off. She’s always up for talking about books, movies, or popular TV shows (but don’t get her started on the ending of How I Met Your Mother—still too soon). She loves to laugh, especially at bad dad jokes and corny puns, and tries to find the humor in everything.

Assistant Director

Sam Shay

Sam is a Communications and Journalism major (not so much the journalism part, however) as well as a Catholic Studies major, and he loves studying each subject. He commonly tells people when they ask why he’s in school that, while he loves talking, he should learn how to do it and when to stop. His other passions are writing, listening to music, running, and video gaming. He is excited about his role in TommComm as Assistant Director, and looks forward to working with the TommComm team!

Project Manager

Monica Gagne

Monica is a Junior at the University of St. Thomas pursuing a major in Communications and Journalism. Her track is leading her towards a field of Advertising and Project Management. After graduating in the winter of 2017 she hopes to get her Project Management certification to work on large corporate campaigns and advertisements.

Originally Monica is from Des Moines, Iowa and moved to the Twin Cities for school and to experience life in a big city. She also participates in extracurricular activities like choir, volunteering, and faith groups. In her free time she likes to do yoga, read, binge watch Netflix shows, and catch up with friends over coffee.
