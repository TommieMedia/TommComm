FAQ

Who can apply for TommComm positions?

Any St. Thomas student is welcome to apply at TommComm! You don't have to be a COJO major’just a hard worker with a passion for advertising.

When does the hiring process begin?

TommComm hires new employees every semester, as well as based on demand. Send us an email if you’d like to receive all of the latest updates.

What positions are open?

TommComm hires students for its entire staff every year, with certain additions scattered here and there. Positions include but are not limited to: Director, Account Manager, Project Manager, and Creative Director.

I’m new to COJO and haven't taken many classes; can I still apply?

Yes! We're always looking for fresh talent, and we keep a list of interested students to contact when openings come up. Even if you feel unqualified, applying and interviewing can be a great, real-life learning experience.

What are the hours and pay like?

Hours vary by position and project status. You may work five hours one week, and then the next. Students are all paid the same hourly wage.
