OUR STORY

Tommie Communications began as an idea presented to a class of St. Thomas students. It didn’t have a vision, a workplace, or even a name. What it did have was 12 dedicated students, strong faculty and community support, and a catchy nickname: TommComm.

Today, TommComm is a full-service student-run communications agency. We partner with real clients on real projects so our employees gain experience in the communication field. And because we aren’t perfect, we have experienced faculty advisers who help us along the way.

TommComm knows that students have talents, knowledge, and perspectives that are valuable to organizations throughout the community. Our work benefits not only our clients, but also the people they serve and our student employees. Helping these simultaneously highlights TommComm’s dedication to advancing the common good.

As a student-run communications agency, TommComm represents today’s creativity by delivering innovative and strategic results in a principled and dependable manner to ensure our clients’ goals become realities.
